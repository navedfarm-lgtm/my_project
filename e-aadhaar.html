e-Aadhaar Download Guide

Step-by-step guide to download e-Aadhaar from the official UIDAI portal.

Steps

  1. Visit the official UIDAI e-Aadhaar page.
  2. Enter Aadhaar/EID and captcha, request OTP.
  3. Verify OTP and download the e-Aadhaar PDF.

Always use the official UIDAI portal to download e-Aadhaar.
